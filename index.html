Connect with golfers in your area

Find a golfing partner with our refined app that connects you with other golf enthusiasts in your area.

Features

Bushwood is a convenient app for connecting with other golfers in your area. It offers advanced matchmaking algorithms, in-app messaging, a GPS-based search function, and the ability to add friends and create group outings. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, Bushwood makes it easy to find and connect with other golfers.

Growth

At Bushwood, we are committed to growing our network of golfers and revolutionizing the sport. By constantly improving and enhancing the app, we aim to increase participation and create a more inclusive community. We are excited about the potential for growth and the positive impact that Bushwood can have on the world of golf

Network

Bushwood is a great platform for networking and making professional connections. Use the app to schedule games and invite colleagues or clients to join you on the course. The shared experience of golf can help build trust and create a sense of camaraderie, making it an ideal environment for building business relationships.

Security

At Bushwood, we take the privacy and security of our users' data very seriously. We have implemented a number of measures to protect user information, including secure servers and encryption technology. We also have strict policies in place to ensure that user data is not shared with third parties without express consent. Rest assured that when you use Bushwood, your data is safe and secure.
